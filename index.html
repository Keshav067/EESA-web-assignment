About EESA

The Electrical Engineering Students' Association (EESA) represents the vibrant student community of the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Bombay. EESA serves as a bridge between students, faculty and alumni by organizing academic, technical, cultural and recreational events throughout the year.
